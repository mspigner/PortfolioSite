BOLTHOUSE FARMS LIVING WALL

To increase awareness for Bolthouse Farms bottled juices, I worked as the lead account manager on the Tiny Rebellion team to install a living outdoor wall in Miami, Florida.

I worked to translate this creative concept into a reality. I found and commission an artist with experience in this specific technique and worked to bring together painters, a team of artists and media vendor to bring this unique vision to life.
The wall was watered twice a day to draw attention to the living greenery.

Rebranding SpeedStick

SITUATION: Gator Gum launched in the 1980s as a delicious gum that tasted just like the real thing. But it lived a short life and was soon pulled off shelves due to declining sales.

OUR APPROACH: Resurrect Gator Gum as Gatorade Gum, a powerful and purposeful gum. Introducing PERFORMANCE GUM - a new category of gum with the active ingredients, and electrolytes. It is the "while" solution, helping replenish what was lost so users can push through.

STRATEGIC FOCUS: Showcase how Gatorade Gum helps users overcome the urge to quit.
