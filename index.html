    Rebranding SpeedStick

    Deodorant brands have a lot to say about what makes a man. For the teenager proving his masculinity to the world, spraying an “X” across his chest helps him project that image.

    But what about the man who does not want his armpits to speak for him? The man who wants to showcase his character, not adopt a persona.

    At the bottom of the shelf is the quiet brand Speedstick. The brand remains just as vanilla as the day our man encountered it in his middle school hygiene kit. But this is its strength. It is purely functional.

    Our man wants his deodorant to be in the background. So it is him, not his Matterhorn Musk, that leaves an impression.

    Strategic Focus: The deodorant for the man who defines himself.

    Reviving Gatorade Gum

    Gator Gum lived a short, but memorable life in the 90s. Our task was to resurrect it. To make it relevant in the labrinth of fitness fuels promoted by fitness gurus and Beachbody coaches.

    To have a shot at success, the gum had to do more than provide a lemon-lime sugar rush. So, we played with the formula and “relaunced” it as PERFORMANCE GUM - a new category of gum with active ingredients and electrolytes.

    PRODUCT PROMISE: Your while solution. It gives you the boost you need without needing to stop to snack or sip.

    TARGET: 18 - 39 year olds who are casually active.
    THEIR NEMESIS: The little voice that whispers “your tired, that’s enough for today.”

    STRATEGIC FOCUS: Showcase how Gatorade Gum helps users overcome the urge to quit.

    Victoria Secret Brand Essence Video

    Victoria’s Secret is femininity.

    But where does femininity fit in a world where women are rewarded professionally and financially when they push their feminine nature aside to approach life with ruthless pragmatism?

    Victoria Secret does not believe that a woman must give up part of herself. She does not have to sacrifice her desire to be delicate in order to be powerful.

    Victoria Secret is about celebrating the intriguing yin and yang that is woman.
